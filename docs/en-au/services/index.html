Art, Culture & History

Art, Culture & History

A charming, timeless coastal town of art and history.

Citadel Saint-Elme

Built in the 16th century, it now houses the Town Hall and several free museums. Its gardens offer a magnificent view of the bay.

Chapel Saint-Pierre

Entirely decorated by artist Jean Cocteau in 1957. A masterpiece dedicated to the fishermen of Villefranche.

Rue Obscure

A real journey back in time. This medieval covered street dates back to 1260 and served as protection for the inhabitants during bombings.

Royal Port of Darse

Former military port of the Dukes of Savoy, with its lighthouse, its historic rope factory, and its 'pointus' (traditional fishing boats).

Explore the French Riviera

An ideal starting point

Located in the heart of the French Riviera, the hotel makes it easy to explore the entire French and Italian Riviera.

  • Nice

    Only 10 minutes away by car or bus. Discover the Promenade des Anglais, Old Nice and its colorful markets.

  • Monaco

    20 minutes away. Dive into the glamour of the Principality, its Prince's Palace and its famous Casino.

  • Eze & Vence

    The famous perched villages of the Riviera. Do not miss Eze Village 15 minutes away, offering a breathtaking panorama.

  • Italie

    The Italian border and the famous market of Ventimiglia are only a 35-minute drive away.
